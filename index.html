⭐ The Little Star Who Never Gave Up ⭐

A Short Story for Kids

🌟 Chapter 1

Once upon a time, there was a little star named Twinkle. Every night, the big stars shined brightly across the sky, but Twinkle's light was small and faint.

Twinkle often wondered, "Will anyone ever notice my light?" The little star felt lonely and wished to shine like the others.

🌙 A Wise Friend

One quiet night, the Moon smiled and said, "Never compare yourself to others. Every star shines in its own special way."

✨ The Big Surprise

One evening, a little child looked up at the sky. Twinkle's gentle light helped the child feel safe during a dark night. At that moment, Twinkle realized that even a small light can bring hope.

❤️ Moral of the Story

Never give up. No matter how small you think you are, your kindness and effort can make a big difference.
